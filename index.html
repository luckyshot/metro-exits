    Barcelona Metro Exits

    English  Español  Català

    Barcelona Metro Exits tells you what Barcelona's tube train carriage number you need to get in so that you are closer to the exit stop you need to go so you save time, queues and extra walking for commutes in the Metro, Ferrocarrils and Funicular of Barcelona.

    How does it work?

    To see what train carriage you need to exit, go to the Search tab and choose the stop you want to exit at. More info

    Editing

    Barcelona Metro Exits is a project maintained by all commuters and users of the app. You can click on Edit to modify information in the app. All edits are manually checked and published in a few hours.
